This Is Who We Are:

How It All Began:

I started writing resumes about 5 years ago. As a student at YearUp NCR (C/O January 2013) they had us create our portfolios,
resumes and cover letters. Although my classmates didn’t seem to really take it too seriously, I knew how important it was to make sure who
you were on paper looked as clean and crisp as the interview day. I started attending free professional development workshops, researching
how to properly set up resume work, and testing all of those skills on myself. Little did I know, I would never find myself struggling to find employment. With the knowledge and experience of the last 5 years, I’ve grown passionate and excited to help others achieve the same
